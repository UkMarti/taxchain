## No income tax states

Alaska, Florida, Nevada, New Hampshire, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Washington, Wyoming — these states have no individual income tax (or have fully repealed it). Skill folders contain sales tax and/or other applicable business taxes only.

- **New Hampshire** repealed its Interest & Dividends Tax effective January 1, 2025. No state income tax filing is required for tax year 2025 and beyond.
- **Tennessee** repealed the Hall Income Tax (on interest and dividends) effective January 1, 2021.

## No sales tax states

Delaware, Montana, New Hampshire, Oregon — these states have no general sales tax. Alaska has no statewide sales tax but allows local jurisdictions to levy one. Skill folders contain income tax and/or other applicable taxes only.

## States with unusual business taxes

| State | Tax | What it is | Affects sole props? |
|-------|-----|-----------|-------------------|
| Washington | B&O Tax | Gross receipts tax on all business activity (no deductions for expenses) | Yes — all businesses |
| Texas | Franchise Tax | Margin tax on entities doing business in TX | LLCs yes, sole props without entity no |
| Ohio | CAT | Commercial Activity Tax on gross receipts (reformed 2024, threshold raised) | Verify — threshold may exclude small businesses |
| Delaware | Gross Receipts Tax | Tax on total gross revenues by business type | Yes — most businesses |
| Nevada | Commerce Tax | Tax on businesses with gross revenue > $4M | Only if revenue > $4M |
| New York | NYC UBT | NYC Unincorporated Business Tax on NYC-based businesses | Yes — if in NYC |

## How to use (end users)

The easiest path: go to `packages/us-[your state code]/` and upload all `.md` files from that folder. The package already includes federal + state skills bundled together. See the [US state index](../../packages/us/README.md).

If you prefer to assemble manually from source:

1. Find your state below
2. Upload ALL `.md` files from your state's folder under `skills/us-states/[code]/`
3. ALSO upload `skills/foundation/us-tax-workflow-base.md` and all files from `skills/federal/`
4. Attach your bank statement and say "Help me with my 2025 taxes"
