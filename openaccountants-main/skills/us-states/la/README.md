## What's NOT covered
- Corporate income tax (Form CIFT-620)
- Corporate franchise tax
- Partnership and S-corp returns (Form IT-565, CIFT-620)
- Part-year and nonresident returns (Form IT-540B)
- Property tax
- Parish-level business taxes
- Amended returns
- Severance taxes (oil, gas, timber)
