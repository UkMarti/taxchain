## What's NOT covered
- Business Profits Tax (8.5% on business income over $75k — applies to businesses, not covered here)
- Business Enterprise Tax (0.55% on enterprise value tax base)
- Meals & Rooms Tax (8.5%)
- Real estate transfer tax
- Property tax (administered at local/municipal level)
- Payroll / employer withholding taxes
- Tobacco, fuel, and other excise taxes
- Estate / inheritance tax (New Hampshire has none)
