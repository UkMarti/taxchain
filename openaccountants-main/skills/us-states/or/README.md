# Oregon — OpenAccountants Tax Skills

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| State | Oregon |
| Code | US-OR |
| Income tax | Yes |
| Sales tax | No — Oregon has no state or local sales tax |
| Franchise / gross receipts tax | Yes — Corporate Activity Tax (CAT) for businesses with >$1M taxable commercial activity |
| Tax authority | [Oregon Department of Revenue](https://www.oregon.gov/dor) |
| Quality tier | Q3 — AI-drafted, not independently verified |

## Files in this folder

| File | What it covers |
|---|---|
| `or-income-tax.md` | Individual income tax for self-employed / sole proprietors (Form OR-40) |

## What's NOT covered
- Payroll / employer withholding taxes (including the Statewide Transit Tax at 0.1%)
- Corporate Activity Tax (CAT) — applies to businesses with >$1M in Oregon taxable commercial activity; $250 + 0.57% of taxable commercial activity over $1M
- Corporate excise/income tax
- Property tax (administered at county level)
- Metro Supportive Housing Services Tax (Multnomah/Clackamas/Washington counties)
- Multnomah County Preschool for All Tax
- Lane Transit District self-employment tax
- Estate tax (Oregon has an estate tax with a $1M exemption)
- TriMet self-employment tax
