## Files in this folder

| File | What it covers |
|---|---|
| `me-income-tax.md` | Individual income tax for self-employed / sole proprietors (Form 1040ME) |
| `me-sales-tax.md` | Sales and use tax obligations and rates |

## What's NOT covered

- Corporate income tax (Form 1120ME)
- Estate tax
- Part-year and non-resident returns (Schedule NR)
- Franchise tax (Maine does not impose one)
- Insurance premiums tax
- Property tax (administered at municipal level)

## How to use

Upload all `.md` files in this folder PLUS the federal skills from `skills/foundation/` and `skills/federal/` to your LLM.
