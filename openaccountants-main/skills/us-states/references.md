# United States — Related Open-Source Projects

OpenAccountants is AGPL-3.0. All projects below have compatible licenses. Projects below can be incorporated with attribution.

## policyengine-us

- Repository: [PolicyEngine/policyengine-us](https://github.com/PolicyEngine/policyengine-us)
- License: AGPL-3.0
- Stars: 145
- Language: Python
- Scope: The definitive US tax-benefit microsimulation model. Covers federal income tax, EITC, CTC, SNAP, Medicaid, SSI, state income taxes for all 50 states, and more.
- Integration: Same AGPL-3.0 license — fully compatible. Federal and state tax computation logic can be directly incorporated.

## py1040

- Repository: [b-k/py1040](https://github.com/b-k/py1040)
- License: GPL-2.0
- Stars: 338
- Language: Python
- Scope: US personal income tax calculator implementing Form 1040. Covers standard/itemized deductions, tax brackets, credits.
- Integration: GPL-2.0 is compatible with AGPL-3.0 (downstream must remain AGPL-3.0). Form 1040 logic and bracket calculations can be adapted.

## Tax-Calculator

- Repository: [PSLmodels/Tax-Calculator](https://github.com/PSLmodels/Tax-Calculator)
- License: Custom open-source
- Stars: 297
- Language: Python
- Scope: USA Federal Individual Income and Payroll Tax Microsimulation Model. Academic-grade tax policy analysis tool.
- Integration: Custom open-source license — verify specific terms before incorporating. Useful as a reference for federal tax policy modeling and validation.
