## Section 1 -- Quick Reference

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Country | Republic of Belarus |
| Currency | BYN (rouble) |
| Headline reality | Individual-entrepreneur regimes are being **restricted**; simplified/unified tax **excluded for IT and many activities**; many IPs must convert to LLC |
| IP general tax | 20% on (income − expenses), or 20% on income; **30%** if annual income > BYN 500,000 |

> **AUDIT FLASH POINT — major transition:** from registration after 1 Oct 2024 and for all existing IPs by **31 Dec 2025**, individual-entrepreneur activity is limited to an **approved list**; IPs outside it must **cease or convert to an LLC**. Do not plan around the old IP simplified regimes without checking this.

## Section 2 -- Individual Entrepreneur (IP) — restricted

- **General system:** 20% on net income (documented expenses) or 20% on income; **30%** above BYN 500,000/yr.
- The **simplified/unified** tax has been narrowed and is **excluded for IT services** and many activities — IT sole proprietors may use **only** the general 20% system.
- Confirm the activity is on the **permitted IP list**, or plan the **conversion to LLC** before 31 Dec 2025.

---

## Section 3 -- Self-Employed (private person, no IP)

- Individuals providing services without IP registration operate under a simplified self-employed scheme.
- **No mandatory** Social Protection Fund contributions (voluntary, to build pension service). Useful for genuinely small/occasional activity. **[RESEARCH GAP — reviewer to confirm the current self-employed activity list and tax amount.]**

---

## Section 4 -- HTP (High-Tech Park) for IT

- HTP residents access a reduced regime (e.g. 9% on certain digital-asset/token revenue) and IT-sector benefits — the **company (HTP-resident LLC)** route, not the restricted IP route, is the way IT businesses optimise. **[RESEARCH GAP — reviewer to confirm current HTP rates and conditions.]**

---

## Section 5 -- Dividends & Capital Gains (2025 changes)

- The preferential **6% and 0%** corporate dividend rates are being **phased out** — model the current rate.
- **Capital gains on Belarusian company shares are now taxed at 13%** (from 1 Jan 2025), removing the prior exemption.

---

## Section 6 -- Red Lines (do not cross)

- **IP activity-list / LLC conversion (31 Dec 2025):** the single biggest compliance item — don't operate outside the permitted list.
- **IT excluded from simplified tax** — IT sole proprietors must use general 20%.
- Don't rely on the lapsing 0%/6% dividend rates.

## PROHIBITIONS

- NEVER advise IT activity under the IP simplified/unified tax — it is excluded (general 20% only).
- NEVER plan around the IP regime without the activity-list / LLC-conversion deadline.
- NEVER present the 0%/6% dividend rates as current.
- NEVER contradict the rates in `belarus-income-tax.md` / `belarus-social-contributions.md`.
