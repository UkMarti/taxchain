> **Albania's headline is a temporary 0% PIT for small businesses:** self-employed individuals and small entities with **gross annual income up to ALL 14 million** (~€135k) pay **0% profit tax until 31 December 2029**. (Tatime; Karanovic)

## Section 2 -- The 0% Small-Business Regime

- **0% PIT** on business/self-employment profit where **gross annual income ≤ ALL 14,000,000**, in force until **31 Dec 2029**.
- The **simplified profit tax** for small business (turnover ≤ ALL 8,000,000) is also at a **0% rate**.
- Above ALL 14m: standard rates apply (progressive PIT 13%/23% bands; corporate income tax). **[RESEARCH GAP — reviewer to confirm the exact above-threshold PIT/CIT rates against `albania-income-tax.md`.]**

This makes Albania extremely efficient for profitable solo/micro service businesses under the cap — but social contributions (~24.4%) and VAT (above ALL 5m) still apply.

---

## Section 3 -- The Freelancer Single-Client Rule (key constraint)

Self-employment income can be **recharacterised** (toward employment-style treatment) if:
- **≥ 80%** of earnings come, directly or indirectly, from **one client**, or
- **≥ 90%** of revenue comes from **fewer than three clients**.

**Exception:** this does **not** apply where the self-employed person supplies services **exclusively to non-resident** individuals/entities without an Albanian permanent establishment.

> **AUDIT FLASH POINT.** A single-client "freelancer" is the primary reclassification target. Diversify clients, or fall within the non-resident exception, and document it.

---

## Section 4 -- Red Lines (do not cross)

- **Single-client / few-client reclassification** (Section 3) — the main risk.
- **VAT registration** is mandatory above ALL 5m turnover — don't ignore it.
- The 0% regime is **time-limited (to 2029)** and income-capped — model the cliff at ALL 14m and the 2030 reversion.
- Don't fragment one business across entities to stay under the cap without substance.
